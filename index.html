About Us

TEJASWINI EDUCATIONAL & WELFARE SOCIETY, founded in 2012, is a dedicated welfare organization committed to empowering individuals through skill-based education and community upliftment. Based in Amalapuram, Andhra Pradesh. we offer:

🧵 Tailoring Training
💻 Computer Training
💄 Beautician Course Training
🏥 Home Care Nursing Training
